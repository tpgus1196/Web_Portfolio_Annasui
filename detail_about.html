Anna Sui is one of New York's most beloved and accomplished fashion designers,known for creating contemporary original clothing inspired by spectacular amounts of research into vintage styles and cultural arcana. Sui joined New York's intensely creative cultural underground in the 1970s. Anna Sui, a first-generation Chinese American came of age in a suburb of Detroit, the career of Anna ui is a classic American success story.

“You have to focus on your dreams,
even if they go beyond common sense.
How could this young girl from the suburbs of Detroit become a success in New York?
It was always that dream,” she says.

Since her premiere runway show in 1991, Sui has produced eighty-four collections, and she remains at the helm of her independent brand. Her label has grown to include accessories, cosmetics, and interiors, and she operates boutiques in the United States and Asia.

ANNA'S FIRST FASHION SHOW

Sui and MEisel attended a Jean Paul Gaultier fashion show in Paris along with Madonna, who surprised Sui by wearing one of her dresses. This gave Sui confidence, and with the encouragement of Meisel, and models Naomi Campbell, Linda Evangelista, and Christy Tulington, she decided to stage her own fashion show. Also in 1991, Sui moved from the design studio in her apartment to her own business premises in the New York Garment District, on Seventh Avenue.
